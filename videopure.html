Pure Browser

- This app is a general-purpose web browser designed to allow users to browse any website. It does not contain any preloaded content, nor does it provide specific features for YouTube or other third-party services. Access to YouTube is entirely based on the user’s input and browsing activity, and the app does not support downloading or modifying any content from YouTube or other websites.
